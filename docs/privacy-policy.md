## English

### About the App

LineLibrary is a mobile application that provides command and shortcut references for software programs.

**Developer:** Ramazan Sarı  
**Contact:** ramoosari@icloud.com

### Data Collection

LineLibrary does **not collect any personal data** from its users.

- ❌ We do not collect personal information
- ❌ We do not collect location data
- ❌ We do not collect usage analytics
- ❌ We do not collect device identifiers
- ❌ We do not share data with third parties

### Local Storage

The app stores the following information **only on your device**:

- Your favorite programs
- Your favorite commands
- Your personal notes
- Your language and theme preferences
- Collections you create

This data is **never** sent to servers or shared with third parties.

### Internet Access

LineLibrary **does not require internet connection** and does not make any network requests.

### Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to this policy will be published on this page.
